Rules

  • On your turn, place the piece your opponent gave you.
  • After placing, choose one remaining piece for your opponent to place next.
  • Win by completing a row, column, or diagonal of four pieces that share one attribute.
  • Shared attributes are tall/short, light/dark, round/square, and solid/hole.
  • If all 16 pieces are placed with no shared-attribute line, the game is a draw.

Piece code: T/S = Tall/Short, L/D = Light/Dark, position 3 R/Q = Round/Square, position 4 F/H = Solid/Hole. Duplicate physical pieces are supported with #slot labels. Squares: A1-D4.
